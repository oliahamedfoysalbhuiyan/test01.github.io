The Company

Barmat Healthcare is a Private Nursing Care Agency. The company was founded with the sole aim of providing specialised care to Service Users in the comfort of their home. A rapidly growing company that boast of an array of care services coupled with fully registered, confident, qualified and experienced staff. With a huge client base scattered all over Greater London, Kent, Essex, Surrey etc. We are fully registered, compliant and regulated by the CQC. We are an equal opportunities employer and embrace equality and diversity.

Founder

The team is led by a Care Quality Commission (CQC) Registered Manager with a vast experience both in public and private healthcare sector. She stands for excellence and is passionate about delivering the highest standards of professional practice. As an NMC Registered nurse, she has been involved with homecare for a number of years delivering a range of services. She has worked for a number of well-known nursing agencies and clients across the country accumulating a wealth of professional and personal experience.
