What is an IVA?
INDIVIDUAL VOLUNTARY AGREEMENT

An IVA is typically suitable for people who have unsecured debts and cannot afford to pay their monthly repayments. IVA eligibility is based on individual circumstances and what you can afford, so if you are eligible a lower debt level can be accepted by your creditors.

The Money Advice Service

When people are struggling with debt it’s more important than ever that they have access to effective advice. Customers can get free debt counselling, debt adjusting and the provision of credit information services from the Money Advice Service – an organisation set up by the Government to offer free and impartial advice to those in debt.
