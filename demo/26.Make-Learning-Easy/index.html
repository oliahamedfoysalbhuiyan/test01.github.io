Undiagnosed Physiological problems may be the answer

You know your child. And they are not flourishing in the existing educational system. You’ve had referrals to various specialists, and your child has been given a label of one sort or another.
Are you frustrated because your child has failed to benefit from mainstream teaching strategies and these specialist interventions? Then isn’t it time to explore another route to see if there is a physical basis underlying their poor academic performance?

1. Sometimes its how they receive information through their sensory channels
2. Sometimes its how they process the sensory information in their brain
3. Sometimes its about under-developed primitive response patterns upon which more complex skills normally build.

Carmel Mullan

left her career as a teacher in mainstream education to pursue her passion to understand the human brain and how it’s development impacts a child’s ability to learn.
