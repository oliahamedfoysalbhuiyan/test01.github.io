about us

Since 1990, the car paradise in Tuning Singen has stood for quality and expertise. This will not change after the takeover at the beginning of 2020. With 5 lifts, 2 offices and 4 employees as well as a trainee, we will continue to be optimally positioned in the future. We also offer you our tire warehouse for 30 euros (with a change of 50 euros).
