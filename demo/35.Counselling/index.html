Affordable Counselling for Individuals, Couples & Families

The Counselling Centre for Hope Healing and Encouragement Inc. provides professional therapeutic counselling for individuals, couples, families and groups in the Westman Region at affordable rates. Providing care in partnership with a number of Employee Assistance Programs (EAP), we offer many different counselling options. For those without insurance coverage, our unique sliding fee scale ensures affordability of our services for all. You won’t be turned away when you come to our centre for help, thanks in part to the generous donations and support we receive from our community.

Committed to Providing Results & Excellent Service

Whether you’re experiencing a speed bump in your marriage or issues coping with a trauma, stress or anger management, our welcoming, compassionate counsellors would like the opportunity to help you get your life back on track. Committed to providing results and excellent service, we strive to improve our clients’ sense of well-being for their environments, as well as their relationships at home, work or other. Many of our clients come to us through referrals and positive word-of-mouth generated by our previous clients.

A Close, Personal Approach

Our compassion and willingness to help others are what drive us to reach out to people who are broken. Proudly offering both healing and encouragement necessary for daily living, we believe our close, personal approach has a positive impact on our clients’ lives. Our professional therapists are highly experienced in a variety of disciplines and offer flexibility in the hours we provide services. For example, we may stay after-hours in a crisis situation without charging the client extra.
